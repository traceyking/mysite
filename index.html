History

The Harley-Davidson Street Bob debuted in 2006 as part of the Dyna line. It was produced as a throwback to the original post-war bobber bikes. In keeping with the "less is more" bobber philosophy, the Harley-Davidson Street Bob featured stripped-down styling void of most decoration and any unnecessary instrumentation in order to accomplish not only a clean look, but a lighter overall weight for faster performance.
